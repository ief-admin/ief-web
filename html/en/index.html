Our Mission

The International Educational Foundation is dedicated to fostering academic excellence, personal growth, and lifelong learning. We believe that a strong educational foundation is rooted in cultural identity and the preservation of one's native language.

By emphasizing learning in a student's native language, we help unlock each child's full potential and pave the way for a brighter, more equitable future for every child.

Inclusive Education

Accessible support for students from Kindergarten through Grade 12.

Cultural Identity

Fostering growth by respecting and utilizing the power of native languages.

Equitable Future

Providing the tools necessary to pave a path toward lifelong success.
