Privacy Policy

International Educational Foundation Inc.

Effective Date: July 1, 2026  |  Last Updated: June 2026

International Educational Foundation Inc. ("IEF," "we," "us," or "our") is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization incorporated in North Carolina, USA. We are committed to protecting the privacy of every person who interacts with our website at ief-global.org. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

By using this website you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use this website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information only when you choose to provide it to us. This may include:

  • Name, email address, mailing address, and telephone number submitted through our contact form or donation process
  • Payment information (processed securely by our third-party payment processor — we do not store card details)
  • Any message or inquiry you send us voluntarily

We do not collect information through passive tracking technologies such as cookies beyond what Cloudflare's infrastructure automatically logs for security and performance purposes (such as server access logs). We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and requests
  • Process and acknowledge your donations
  • Send program updates, newsletters, or impact reports (only if you have opted in)
  • Comply with US legal and IRS tax-reporting requirements for charitable contributions
  • Improve our website and communications

We will not use your information for any commercial purpose or for purposes unrelated to IEF's mission.

3. How We Protect Your Information

IEF treats all personal and financial information as strictly confidential. We maintain the following safeguards:

  • Personal data is stored in secured systems protected by authentication controls
  • Payment transactions are processed through a PCI-compliant payment provider with SSL/TLS encryption
  • Access to donor information is restricted to authorized staff and volunteers on a need-to-know basis
  • We periodically review our data practices to ensure continued security

4. Information Sharing

IEF does not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third party, except:

  • With our payment processor, solely to complete your donation transaction
  • With our email service provider, if you have opted in to receive communications from us
  • As required by law, court order, or government authority

We do not share donor email addresses, phone numbers, or mailing addresses with any outside organization for any marketing or solicitation purpose.

5. Donor Confidentiality

IEF deeply values every contribution to our mission. As a donor, you have the right to:

  • Be informed of how your donated resources are used
  • Have access to our most recent financial statements
  • Receive timely and appropriate acknowledgment of your gift
  • Opt out of future mailings or newsletters at any time by contacting us at info@ief-global.org
  • Request that your name not be published in donor lists or public materials

All donor information is handled with confidentiality to the full extent permitted by law.

6. Donations from Outside the United States

If you are an Indian passport holder residing outside India, your donation will be treated per applicable IRS and Indian regulations. Please contact us at info@ief-global.org before donating so we can provide guidance on documentation requirements.

If you are a non-US, non-Indian passport holder, please contact us to confirm your donation can be accepted under applicable law.

7. Children's Privacy

IEF's educational programs serve children in Tamil Nadu, India. However, this website is intended for adults — donors, supporters, volunteers, and partners. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 through this website.

If a parent or guardian believes that a child under 13 has submitted personal information to us through this website, please contact us immediately at info@ief-global.org and we will promptly delete the information.

8. Cookies and Analytics

This website is hosted on Cloudflare. Cloudflare may use technically necessary cookies for security and performance. We do not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or any other behavioral tracking or advertising technology. No personal data is sold to or shared with advertisers.

9. Data Retention

We retain donor records for a minimum of seven (7) years as required by IRS regulations for 501(c)(3) organizations. Contact information used for communications will be retained until you request removal. You may request deletion of your information at any time, subject to our legal retention obligations.

10. Changes to This Policy

IEF reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the website following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
